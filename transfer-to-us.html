Transfering to Us

Transferring Fostering Agencies

  • Unhappy with your current agency? Transfer to CuteChamp and enjoy the amazing benefits of working as part of our friendly team.
  • We can help you through the transfer process, whether you have current placements or not.
  • You will receive unparalleled support from our supervising social workers and team as well as other benefits with our carer support package.

Why transfer to CuteChamp Fostering?

At CuteChamp Fostering, we welcome existing and experienced Foster Parents to come and join our friendly agency. Many people may not realise that you can switch provider. There may be many reasons that you wish to transfer foster agency. Perhaps you are moving house, are unhappy with the levels of support or training you receive, or are looking for a new challenge? Being happy with your agency is a very important factor and happy foster parents make for happy children! We are completely dedicated to the safeguarding of children and young people. We see our foster carers as an invaluable part of this duty and we value the work that they do. We do all we can to support and empower our carers to allow them to be the absolute best carers they can be. We promise to provide full training with 24/7 support and regular opportunities to grow, develop and learn. We support you 100% and we treat you as a valuable and skilled member of the CuteChamp Fostering team. Caring for your future and that of the children we support.

The guidelines are intended to ensure the ongoing care and safety of children and young people during the process of switching as well as empowering carers.

The process differs slightly depending on whether you currently have a child in your care at the time you decide to switch.

Transferring when you have a foster child placement

The first step is to contact us and make your intentions to move agency known. We can discuss your current placements and what you can expect from CuteChamp Fostering. You will also need to contact your existing agency as well as the placing Local Authority of the child placed in your care and inform them of your decision of intent to transfer agency. Once this has been formalised, CuteChamp will organise a transfer of protocol meeting with all of the relevant parties. This will include you.

Once a formal decision has been made about the child transferring over with you to CuteChamp, you can begin the application process with us. Although it consists of the same steps as your initial application to foster, it is much more streamlined and will hopefully take less time!

Transferring when you donâ€™t have a foster child placement

You will need to give notice of intent to your current foresting agency to transfer to CuteChamp as soon as you have decided to move. You will be able to apply to foster with us straight away. We will go through the normal process of a home visit to get to know you and we will ask for a reference from your current agency. You will be supported throughout the entire transition process by one of our social workers.
